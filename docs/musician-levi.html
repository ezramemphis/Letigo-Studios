Levi Bennett

He plays all saxes, flute, and clarinet

Levi Bennett is a versatile woodwind player with a deep love for expressive, soulful melodies and tight ensemble work. His background spans classical training and jazz improvisation, giving him the flexibility to adapt to any musical situation. Whether laying down buttery smooth tenor lines or electrifying a solo with alto fire, Levi brings unmatched passion and tone to every performance. He thrives in collaboration, always listening and blending to lift the track's emotional core. A true craftsman of the breath-driven voice, Levi continues to explore the full sonic potential of his instruments in every session.
