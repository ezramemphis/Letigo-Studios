Mixing With Intention

Looking for a mixing/recording engineer that will chase your artistic vision with you?

Letigo Studios is a creative space led by Ezra — a producer, mixing engineer, and multi-instrumentalist passionate about capturing authentic performances and elevating artists’ visions. With roots in R&B, hip-hop, and indie, Ezra blends musicality and technical expertise to deliver mixes that feel alive.

Our mission is simple: to be your trusted partner in bringing your ideas to life, whether in-person or remotely, with intention and care. At Letigo, every project is about more than sound — it’s about crafting a sonic journey that truly represents you.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At our studio, music isn’t just sound—it’s a story. Every project is approached as a unique artistic journey, where creative vision drives every decision.

We believe in crafting mixes and productions that reflect the artist’s personality while meeting the highest technical standards of the industry.

Whether we’re recording, mixing, or producing, the goal is the same: to bring out the emotion and energy that makes your music unforgettable.

OUR APPROACH

We combine the warmth and depth of analog gear with the precision of modern digital tools, ensuring every project has both character and clarity.

Collaboration is at the heart of our process—we listen, adapt, and work closely with you to bring your ideas to life.

Our team is dedicated to making the studio environment creative, inspiring, and comfortable, so you can focus on what matters most: making great music.
